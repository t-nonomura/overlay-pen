# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-01

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android app "最前面ペン | 画面に描ける注釈ツール" (English name: Overlay Pen / Screen Pen, the "App").

## 1. Overview

The App provides a floating drawing and annotation tool that lets users draw over the screen.  
The App is designed to minimize the collection of user data wherever possible.

## 2. Data We Collect

At this time, the App does not send, collect, or share the following data with the developer's servers:

- Personal information such as name, address, phone number, or email address
- Account information
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos, videos, audio, or other media files
- Advertising ID
- Analytics data
- Purchase information

## 3. Data Processed on Device

The App temporarily handles drawing-related state in device memory while the App is in use.  
This data is not transmitted off the device, not shared with third parties, and is cleared when the drawing session ends.

## 4. Permissions and Device Features

- Overlay-related permission to display drawing controls over other apps
- Foreground service to keep the overlay active and stable while in use

## 5. Data Sharing

The App does not sell, share, or provide user data to third parties.  
At this time, the App also does not include advertising, analytics, billing, or crash reporting SDKs.
